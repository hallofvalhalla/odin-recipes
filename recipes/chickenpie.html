chicken pot pie

This recipe is going to show you how to make an american classic

the chicken pot pie!

ingredients

  • chicken breast
  • cream of potato soup
  • mixed veggies
  • pie shell

Steps

  1. place the pie shell in a round pan
  2. mix soup veggies and chicken together
  3. add into pie shell
  4. add second pie shell on top and close
  5. cut three slits in the top
  6. bake at 350 fahrenheit for 45 minutes
