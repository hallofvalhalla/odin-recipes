Lasagna

description

today we will be helping you create a true italian classic.

Lasagna! This dish will have you wishing you could make it sooner

Ingredients

  • pasta sheets
  • red sauce
  • ricotta cheese
  • italian spices
  • meat

Steps

  1. place pasta sheets on bottom of sheet pan
  2. add ricotta cheese
  3. add meat
  4. add more pasta sheets over top
  5. bake at 350 fahrenheit for 45 minutes to an hour
