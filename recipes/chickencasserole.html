chicken casserole

This recipe is going to help you create a delish and creamy chicken dish that is perfect for the whole family. With this recipe we will help you create an incredible dish.

Ingredients

  • chicken breast
  • cream of mushroom soup
  • green beans
  • rice
  • chedder cheese
  • mayo
  • milk

Steps

  1. cook the chicken and the rice
  2. add rice to the bottom of an 8x10 sheet pan
  3. add chicken and green beans on top of the rice
  4. mix mayo, milk, and cream of mushroom soup
  5. put mayo mix over the chicken and green beans
  6. add chedder cheese to cover
  7. add bread crumb topping
  8. bake at 350 fahrenheit for 45 minutes
